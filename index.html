Mengqian Zhang (张梦倩)

I am a third-year PhD student from Department of Computer Science and Engineering of Shanghai Jiao Tong University and my advisor is Prof. Xiaotie Deng.

Before this, I got the B.S. degree in computer science from Ocean University of China in 2018.

Since July 2019, I have been a visiting scholar at Center on Frontiers of Computing Studies, Peking University.

My research interest has been mainly in blockchain and algorithmic game theory.
