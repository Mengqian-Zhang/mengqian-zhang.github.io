Mengqian Zhang (张梦倩)

I am currently a postdoctoral fellow at Stern School of Business of New York University, hosted by Prof. Xi Chen and Prof. Elynn Chen.

I got my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, and I was very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Xiaotie Deng. Prior to that, I got the B.E. degree in Computer Science from Ocean University of China in 2018.

I was a visiting scholar at Center on Frontiers of Computing Studies, Peking University from July 2019 to December 2022.

My research interests mainly focus on blockchain and algorithmic economics.
